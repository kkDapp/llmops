EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK — ACME CORP
Version 2.0 | Effective January 2025

========================================
SECTION 1: PAID HOLIDAYS
========================================

Acme Corp observes 12 paid public holidays per calendar year. All full-time employees
are entitled to these days off with full pay.

Official Public Holidays (2025):
  1. New Year's Day          — January 1
  2. Martin Luther King Day  — January 20
  3. Presidents' Day         — February 17
  4. Memorial Day            — May 26
  5. Independence Day        — July 4
  6. Labor Day               — September 1
  7. Columbus Day            — October 13
  8. Veterans Day            — November 11
  9. Thanksgiving Day        — November 27
 10. Day after Thanksgiving  — November 28
 11. Christmas Eve           — December 24
 12. Christmas Day           — December 25

If a holiday falls on a Saturday, it is observed on the preceding Friday.
If a holiday falls on a Sunday, it is observed on the following Monday.

========================================
SECTION 2: ANNUAL LEAVE (VACATION)
========================================

Employees accrue paid vacation based on years of service:

  0–2 years:    15 days per year  (1.25 days/month)
  3–5 years:    20 days per year  (1.67 days/month)
  6–10 years:   25 days per year  (2.08 days/month)
  10+ years:    30 days per year  (2.50 days/month)

Vacation days must be approved by your manager at least 2 weeks in advance.
A maximum of 10 unused days may be carried over to the next calendar year.
Unused days beyond the carry-over limit are forfeited on December 31.

========================================
SECTION 3: SICK LEAVE
========================================

All employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year.
Sick days do not carry over to the next year.
A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

========================================
SECTION 4: PERSONAL DAYS
========================================

Employees receive 3 personal days per calendar year for personal matters,
family emergencies, or religious observances not covered by public holidays.
Personal days must be requested at least 24 hours in advance when possible.

========================================
SECTION 5: BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
========================================

Paid bereavement leave is provided as follows:
  - Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days
  - Extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt/uncle): 3 days
  - Close friend or colleague: 1 day

========================================
SECTION 6: PARENTAL LEAVE
========================================

Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid parental leave.
Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks fully paid parental leave.
Parental leave can be taken any time within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

========================================
SECTION 7: TOTAL LEAVE SUMMARY
========================================

At a glance — a mid-career employee (3–5 years) receives:
  - 12 public holidays
  - 20 vacation days
  - 10 sick days
  - 3 personal days
  -------------------------
  Total: 45 days off per year (excluding parental/bereavement)

========================================
SECTION 8: LEAVE REQUEST PROCESS
========================================

1. Submit request via the HR portal (hr.acmecorp.com)
2. Manager approves or declines within 3 business days
3. Calendar invite sent to the team automatically
4. For sick leave: notify your manager before 9 AM on the day of absence

For questions, contact HR at hr@acmecorp.com or ext. 4200.
